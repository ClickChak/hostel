Welcome to Wall Maria

Experience luxury and comfort at affordable prices in our carefully designed spaces. Located in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal, we offer the perfect blend of comfort and convenience.

Our hostel is designed to cater to the needs of travelers from all walks of life. Whether you're a solo backpacker, a couple seeking a romantic getaway, or a family on vacation, we have the perfect accommodations for you.

Explore the vibrant city of Kathmandu from our centrally located hostel. Visit nearby attractions such as the Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, and the bustling streets of Thamel.

Enjoy our range of facilities, including free WiFi, a communal kitchen, and a cozy common room where you can meet fellow travelers. Start your day with a delicious breakfast and unwind in the evening with a cup of coffee in our lounge area.

At Wall Maria Hostel, we prioritize your safety and comfort. Our premises are equipped with 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance. We also offer secure parking for guests traveling by car or motorcycle.
